Products


Graphite Crucibles more_vert

Graphite Crucibles close

  Graphite crucibles are containers that are used to melt different metals like gold, silver, aluminium, brass, gunmetal and copper at corresponding temperature. It is made by natural graphite raw material.

Silicon Crucibles more_vert

Silicon Crucibles close

  Silicon Carbide Crucibles are made up of carbon bonded silicon and graphite material. It withstand the temperature up to 1600 degree. It is used for melting the Gold, silver, Brass, Aluminium and cast iron. It is also used for melting the zinc for production of zinc oxide. It has advantages of high thermal shock capacity, High capacity, Low energy consumption and high conductivity.

Silicon Carbide Stand more_vert

Silicon Carbide Stand close

  Silicon Carbide Stand is used to support the crucibles by giving fully supporting flat surface with high conduction and thermal expansion rate that is similar to the crucibles. These stands have different dimensions and shape which depends on the application.

Foundry flux more_vert

Foundry flux close

  Foundry flux is used to remove impurities like oxidation losses in metals during melting. It improves the metal quality and recovery rate of the metal. It reduces the inclusions and loss of metal during castings.

Foundry Diecoat more_vert

Foundry Diecoat close

  Diecoat is a chemical that is used to smooth the outer surface of the molten metal. It gives better outer cover and controls solidification. This will stick with the molten metal.

Degassing Tablets more_vert

Degassing Tablets close

  Degassing tablets are additives used to remove the gasses in all varieties of alloys. When the tablets dissolves with the molten metal, the tablets produces chlorine which removes all types of dissolved hydrogen and other gases in the metal.

Modifier Flux more_vert

Modifier Flux close

  Modifier is used to remove oxide formation and other non-metallic impurities from the alloys. Especially in sand castings, in order to maintain the mechanical properities the alloy should be modified prior to castings. It is in blue colour. It can be used as protective flux.

Metal Putty more_vert

Metal Putty close

Metal Putty (Aluminium, Brass and gun metal) is used to cover the pin holes, cracks and any cosmetic problems in casting. It is very strong with good adhesion property. It withstands high temperature. It is used to improve the outer look of any metal. After applying the putty, the metal looks like perfect finishing with smooth and soft surface.
