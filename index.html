About Us


    SATRON engineering was established in 2005. We started analyzing products used in the foundry industry. Due to the demand for good quality raw materials, we planned to have a company that deals with quality material in the foundry industry. We have suppliers and customers all over INDIA. Our company has more than 15 years of experience in this field. We supply and export quality crucibles, flux, die coat, metal putty, and foundry raw materials. Our motto is to expand our services to all types of foundry products and businesses worldwide.
